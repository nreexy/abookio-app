Abookio Privacy Policy

Effective Date:26 October 2025

1. Introduction

Abookio is a privacy-first audiobook player that allows you to listen to your personal audiobook collection from local files or your own cloud storage. We do not collect, share, or sell personal data.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Abookio does not collect or transmit any personal data, usage analytics, or identifiers to external servers or third parties.

3. Local Data Storage

Your audiobook metadata, playback progress, bookmarks, notes, and collections are stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers. This data remains private to you.

4. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync (Abookio Pro feature), your audiobook metadata, progress, bookmarks, and notes are synced securely via Apple’s iCloud using end-to-end encryption. No other servers have access to this data.

5. Cloud Provider Connections

Abookio Pro users can connect their own cloud storage providers (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or custom providers using a URL and API key). Connections use read-only access and are authenticated directly with the provider’s official login flow or user-provided credentials. Abookio does not have access to or store your cloud files.

6. Purchases

Abookio Pro is available as a one-time in-app purchase through the Apple App Store. All purchase processing is handled by Apple, and Abookio does not store payment or user account information.

7. Email Communication

If you contact us via email at support@abookio.com, your message and address are retained for up to 12 months for support purposes and then deleted automatically.

8. User Rights

You can delete all local and iCloud-synced data at any time from within the app. No data remains on our systems, as we do not operate any user data servers.

9. Children’s Privacy

Abookio is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact: Email: support@abookio.com
