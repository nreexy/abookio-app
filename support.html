Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my audiobooks?
You can import local audiobook files directly from your device or connect your own cloud storage provider (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or a custom provider using a URL + API key).

Does Abookio upload my files?
No. All your audiobook files and related data stay on your device or in your personal cloud storage. Nothing is uploaded to Abookio servers.

What is synced with iCloud?
If you enable iCloud Sync (Pro feature), only metadata, playback progress, bookmarks, and notes are securely synced via Apple’s iCloud with end-to-end encryption.

Do you collect analytics or track users?
No. Abookio does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party advertising tools.
