<p>Welcome to Abookio! This guide will help you get set up.</p>

<h2>Installation</h2>
<p>Download Abookio from the <a href="https://apps.apple.com/app/6754542041">App Store</a>.</p>

<h2>First Steps</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Open the app.</li>
  <li>Tap on "Add Cloud Account".</li>
  <li>Select your provider (iCloud, Dropbox, etc.).</li>
  <li>Select the folder containing your audiobooks.</li>
</ol>

<p>Abookio will scan your folder and present your library beautifully.</p>
